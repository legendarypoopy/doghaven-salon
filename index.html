THE EXPERTS INSIDE

OUR SALON

Whether it is a pampering full grooming, simple bathing or trimming, we'll customise the most suitable grooming needs for your pet at your request. The spacious, clean and well-equipped grooming salon promises to make your furkid parasite-free. Our cage-less haven is guaranteed to make them extra calm and more comfortable throughout the entire grooming process.

All furkids records such as past illness, surgeries, dental care, veterinarian care recommendation and special requirement are filed and updated regularly.

In-house grooming operates daily from Tuesay to Sunday, except public holidays.
